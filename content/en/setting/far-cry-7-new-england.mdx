<Section icon={Target} title="The 72-Hour Campaign Timer & Interrogation Mechanics">

Project Blackbird introduces a massive structural shift to the franchise's traditional open-world formula. Instead of slowly liberating regions at your own pace, the campaign is built around a persistent, high-stakes countdown.

### Real-Time vs. In-Game Time
The central campaign tasks players with rescuing members of the wealthy Beckett family. This rescue mission must be completed within a strict limit.

| Time System | Duration | Campaign Impact |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **In-Game Timer** | 72 Hours | Pauses only inside designated safehouses |
| **Real-Time equivalent** | ~24 Hours | Active gameplay, combat, and exploration |
