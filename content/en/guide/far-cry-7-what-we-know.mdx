<Section icon={Target} title="The Beckett Family Story and Non-Linear Campaign">

The narrative structure of Project Blackbird represents a massive departure from previous entries. Instead of fighting to liberate a fictional country from a dictator, players are thrust into a personal crisis. The plot centers on the wealthy **Beckett Family**, who have been targeted and kidnapped by a dangerous conspiracy cult known as the **Sons of Truth**.

The campaign is built around a non-linear rescue mission where you must track down and save your relatives. Saving every family member is required to achieve 100% campaign completion, and the death of specific family members will dynamically alter the ending of the story.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Timer and Interrogation Mechanics">

The most talked-about leak surrounding Far Cry 7 is the introduction of a persistent **72-hour in-game timer**. This translates to approximately **24 hours of real-world gameplay**. The countdown is displayed on the protagonist's wristwatch, forcing players to make critical strategic decisions about when to explore, plan, or strike.

To find the locations of the kidnapped Beckett family members, players must utilize a brand-new **Interrogation System**. Unlike previous games where information was handed out automatically, interrogating enemies in Project Blackbird requires intuition and risk management.

| Interrogation Outcome | Description | Player Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **The Truth** | Enemy cooperates and gives exact coordinates. | Unlocks a direct rescue mission on the map. |
| **Deception** | Enemy lies and provides false coordinates. | Wastes valuable time investigating a dead end. |
| **Silence** | Enemy refuses to speak or breaks under pressure. | Yields zero information; requires finding new leads. |
| **Escape** | Enemy slips away during the interrogation. | Alerts nearby cult outposts to your presence. |

<Callout type="success" title="Safehouse Mechanic">
Entering an unlocked safehouse completely pauses the 72-hour campaign timer. This allows players to manage inventory, craft upgrades, and plan routes without losing precious time.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Project Blackbird vs. Project Maverick">

Ubisoft is developing two distinct Far Cry projects simultaneously. It is crucial to understand the differences between the mainline campaign (Project Blackbird) and the standalone multiplayer project (Project Maverick). 

While both games share foundational movement mechanics and asset libraries, they offer completely different gameplay loops and target audiences.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Technical Upgrades and Snowdrop Engine">

For years, the Far Cry series relied heavily on the Dunia engine. However, development reports indicate that Ubisoft is transitioning the franchise to the **Snowdrop engine** (previously used for *The Division* and *Star Wars Outlaws*). This engine shift introduces superior physics, advanced lighting, and highly responsive movement systems.

The movement mechanics have been completely overhauled to support tactical, fast-paced traversal across the open world.
