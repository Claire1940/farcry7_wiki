<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the 72-Hour Campaign Timer">

The defining mechanic of our **far cry 7 walkthrough** is the persistent countdown. Players are granted 72 hours of in-game time to complete the main story. This translates to roughly 24 hours of real-world gameplay. Unlike previous titles where you could spend dozens of hours hunting side activities without consequence, every minute in Project Blackbird counts toward the survival of your family members.

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Pausing">
The countdown only pauses when you are inside a **Safehouse**. Use these locations to manage your gear, craft upgrades, and study gathered intel without losing precious time.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the Wristwatch">
    Your character wears a physical wristwatch that displays the remaining time. Check this frequently to decide if you have enough time for a side objective or if you must head directly to a rescue point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize High-Value Intel">
    Not all missions provide the same rewards. Focus on "Intel Leads" that reveal the exact location of a Beckett family member.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Safehouse Rotation">
    Identify and unlock Safehouses in each major district. These act as your only reprieve from the ticking clock and are vital for fast travel and healing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Rescue">
    Once a location is confirmed, move quickly. If the timer reaches zero before a family member is saved, they may be moved or permanently lost, altering the game's ending.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Story and Faction Guide: The Sons of Truth">

The narrative of Far Cry 7 centers on the **Beckett family**, a wealthy group targeted by a conspiracy cult known as the **Sons of Truth**. This group isn't just a military force; they are an organization obsessed with hallucinogenic experiments and psychological warfare. In this **far cry 7 walkthrough**, understanding your enemy is just as important as your aim.

The Sons of Truth utilize a decentralized structure. Instead of one main villain, you will face various high-ranking members who hold different Beckett family members captive. Their tactics include setting traps, using decoys, and feeding you false information during interrogations.

<Callout type="info" title="Nonlinear Narrative">
The order in which you rescue your family members changes the dialogue and the final outcome of the story. Rescuing the youngest members first might provide different tactical advantages than saving the patriarch.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Interrogation Mechanics and Intel Gathering">

A core pillar of any successful **far cry 7 walkthrough** is the interrogation system. To find your family, you must capture and question enemy officers. However, the system is designed to be unpredictable. Unlike previous games where information was always 100% accurate, enemies in Far Cry 7 can and will lie to you.

<Callout type="warning" title="Truth vs. Deception">
An interrogated enemy might give you the correct coordinates, stay silent, provide a trap location, or even try to escape while you are distracted.
</Callout>

| Response Type | Likelihood | Tactical Advice |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Verified Truth** | 40% | Cross-reference with other intel sources |
| **Direct Lies** | 30% | Scout the area from a distance before entering |
| **Silence** | 20% | Use environmental pressure or let them go for tracking |
| **Escape Attempt** | 10% | Keep your weapon drawn during the process |
