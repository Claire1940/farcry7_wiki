<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics: The 72-Hour Timer">

The most significant departure from traditional franchise design is the rumored integration of a real-time countdown. The rescue campaign must be completed before the clock runs out, forcing players to make strategic decisions about exploration, combat, and resource gathering.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Track the Countdown">
    The player character wears a physical wristwatch displaying a 72-hour in-game timer, which translates to approximately 24 hours of real-time gameplay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Safehouses">
    Entering designated safehouses pauses the countdown. This allows players to manage their inventory, craft gear, and plan their next route without losing active time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Intelligence">
    Locate and interrogate cult members to secure reliable coordinates. Players must weigh the time spent traveling against the validity of the information obtained.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Nonlinear Rescues">
    Infiltrate enemy strongholds to rescue family members. Because there is no fixed order, players can prioritize targets based on proximity or the difficulty of the region.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Blackbird vs. Maverick: Two Separate Projects">

To understand the current state of development, it is essential to distinguish between the two separate projects currently in production at Ubisoft. While both share technical foundations, they target entirely different gaming audiences.

*   **Project Blackbird**: The next mainline single-player (and cooperative) campaign, commonly referred to in community discussions as Far Cry 7.
*   **Project Maverick**: A standalone, multiplayer-focused experience centered around extraction-style survival elements.

| Feature | Project Blackbird (Mainline Campaign) | Project Maverick (Multiplayer) |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary Focus** | Narrative-driven rescue mission | Extraction-based survival shooter |
| **Game Setting** | To be announced | Fictional Alaskan wilderness |
| **Opponents** | AI-controlled cult factions | AI soldiers and rival human players |
| **Core Loop** | Manage timer, interrogate, and rescue | Gather loot, complete contracts, and extract |
| **Progression** | Story-based family completion | Hideout upgrades and persistent gear storage |
