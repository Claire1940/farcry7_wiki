<Section icon={Users} title="Project Maverick: The Multiplayer Expansion">

Parallel to the mainline game, Ubisoft is developing **Project Maverick**. This is a standalone multiplayer experience that leans heavily into the "extraction shooter" genre. While the **far cry 7 release date 2027** target applies to the single-player game, Maverick may enter beta testing much earlier.

**Key Features of Project Maverick:**
- **Setting**: A fictionalized Alaskan wilderness featuring extreme weather.
- **Gameplay**: Permadeath mechanics where players lose gear upon dying in the zone.
- **Progression**: A shared "Hideout" where players store loot and upgrade their base.
- **Contract System**: Players take on missions to extract specific resources or eliminate AI targets.
