<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Snowdrop Engine Upgrades on PS5">

One of the most significant technical shifts for **far cry 7 ps5** is the reported abandonment of the Dunia engine. Dunia has powered the franchise's tropical, mountainous, and rural landscapes since Far Cry 2. Instead, Project Blackbird is utilizing the **Snowdrop engine**, which powers titles like *The Division* and *Star Wars Outlaws*.

This transition aims to maximize the hardware capabilities of the PlayStation 5, focusing on seamless asset streaming, advanced physics, and complex environmental destruction.

<Section icon={Target} title="The 72-Hour Gameplay Timer & Interrogation Mechanics">

Project Blackbird reportedly introduces a non-linear narrative structure centered around a kidnapped wealthy family, the Becketts. Unlike previous games where you slowly liberate a map sector by sector, Far Cry 7 tasks you with rescuing your family members within a strict time limit.

The campaign features a **72-hour in-game timer**, which translates to roughly **24 hours of real-time gameplay**. This clock is displayed on your character's wristwatch, pressure-cooking every decision you make in the open world.

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plan Your Rescue Routes">
