<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Project Blackbird: Development and Engine Transition">

The development of the next Far Cry marks a significant technical shift for the franchise. For over a decade, the series has utilized the Dunia engine. However, credible reports from industry insiders suggest that **far cry 7** is being built on the **Snowdrop engine**. This is the same technology powering *The Division* series and *Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora*, known for its superior lighting, environmental destruction, and seamless open-world streaming.

This transition is intended to "reinvent" the series formula, which some critics felt had become stagnant. By moving to Snowdrop, Ubisoft Montreal aims to implement more complex gameplay systems that were previously limited by the aging Dunia architecture.

| Feature | Dunia Engine (Old) | Snowdrop Engine (New) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Physics** | Scripted/Limited | Dynamic/Systemic |
| **Lighting** | Static/Pre-baked | Fully Dynamic Global Illumination |
| **World Size** | Large but Segmented | Massive/Seamless |
| **AI Systems** | Basic Pathfinding | Advanced Behavioral Logic |
