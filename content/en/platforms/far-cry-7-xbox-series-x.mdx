<Section icon={Users} title="Project Blackbird vs. Project Maverick">

It is essential to distinguish between the two Far Cry projects currently in development. **Project Blackbird** is the mainline single-player (with co-op) experience known as Far Cry 7. **Project Maverick**, on the other hand, is a standalone multiplayer project focused on extraction-style gameplay.

Maverick is reportedly set in a fictional **Alaskan wilderness**, featuring survival mechanics, permanent death (perk/gear loss), and a shared hideout system. While the two games share some technical assets and movement systems (like tactical sprinting and sliding), they are being designed as distinct products to satisfy different segments of the player base.

| Feature | Project Blackbird (Far Cry 7) | Project Maverick (Multiplayer) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
